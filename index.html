Palm Care Preview

🌱

Planting

Dig wide, keep the crown high, and water to settle the root ball.

💧

Watering

Deep, infrequent irrigation builds stronger roots in desert conditions.

🧪

Fertilizing

Use a palm-safe blend with micronutrients to prevent deficiencies.

Queen Palm Frizzle Top Watch

Yellowing new fronds and twisted growth can signal manganese deficiency. Choose the right fertilizer, avoid overwatering, and call us for guidance if you suspect stress.
